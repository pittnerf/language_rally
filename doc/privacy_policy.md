# Privacy Policy — Language Rally

**Effective date: March 20, 2026**

---

## 1. Overview

Language Rally ("the App") is a personal language learning application developed as a solo project. Protecting your privacy is a core principle of how the App is designed. In short: **the App does not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data with the developer or any third party — unless you explicitly choose to use optional third-party API features.**

---

## 2. Data Stored on Your Device

All data created or managed within the App is stored **locally on your device only**:

| Data | Where it is stored |
|---|---|
| Vocabulary packages (words, expressions, categories) | Local SQLite database |
| Training history and learning statistics | Local SQLite database |
| App preferences and UI settings | Local device storage (SharedPreferences) |
| Your DeepL API key (if provided) | Local device storage (SharedPreferences) |
| Your OpenAI API key (if provided) | Local device storage (SharedPreferences) |
| Custom package icon images | Local file system |

None of this data is transmitted to the developer, to any analytics service, or to any advertising network.

---

## 3. No Account or Registration Required

Language Rally does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

---

## 4. Optional Third-Party API Features

Certain premium features use external APIs. These are **entirely optional**, require you to supply your own API keys, and are only activated when you explicitly trigger those features.

### 4a. DeepL Translation API
- **Provider:** DeepL SE, Maarweg 165, 50825 Cologne, Germany
- **When used:** Only when you request a translation within the App
- **What is sent:** The word, phrase, or sentence you wish to translate, along with your target and source language codes
- **What is NOT sent:** Your identity, device information, or any other personal data
- **DeepL Privacy Policy:** https://www.deepl.com/privacy

### 4b. OpenAI API
- **Provider:** OpenAI, L.L.C., 3180 18th Street, San Francisco, CA 94110, USA
- **When used:** Only when you request AI-generated example sentences or vocabulary extraction from a text
- **What is sent:** The word, phrase, or text you submit for processing
- **What is NOT sent:** Your identity, device information, or any other personal data
- **OpenAI Privacy Policy:** https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

> ⚠️ **Important:** Your API keys are stored exclusively on your device. The App never transmits your API keys to the developer or any other party. Each key is sent directly from your device to the respective API provider (DeepL / OpenAI) as part of the standard authentication flow for that service.

---

## 5. Device Permissions

The App requests the following device permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Microphone** | To record your voice during pronunciation practice sessions |
| **Speech Recognition** | To evaluate your spoken responses during language training |
| **Internet** | To communicate with the DeepL and OpenAI APIs when you use those features |
| **File Access** | To import and export vocabulary packages at your request |

Microphone access is used only during an active pronunciation or recording session initiated by you. Audio is processed locally or via the device's speech recognition system and is not stored permanently or transmitted to the developer.

---

## 6. Import and Export

The App allows you to export vocabulary packages as ZIP files and import them from files on your device or shared by others. This functionality is entirely under your control. No files are uploaded to any server operated by the developer.

---

## 7. Analytics and Advertising

Language Rally contains **no analytics code**, **no advertising SDKs**, and **no crash-reporting services**. The developer does not receive any telemetry or usage statistics from the App.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). Since the App does not collect personal data from any user, no special measures beyond standard parental guidance are needed.

---

## 9. Data Security

Because all data remains on your device, its security is governed by your device's own security mechanisms (screen lock, encryption, etc.). Your API keys are stored using the platform's standard secure local storage. The developer has no access to your data and therefore cannot breach, leak, or misuse it.

---

## 10. Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected or processed by the developer, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete on our side. You are in full control of your data:

- **Delete app data:** Uninstall the App or clear app data through your device settings.
- **Delete API keys:** Remove them at any time from the Settings section within the App.
- **Delete packages:** Remove any vocabulary package directly from within the App.

---

## 11. Changes to This Policy

If a future version of the App introduces new features that affect data privacy, this policy will be updated and the new effective date will be noted at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after a policy change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

---

## 12. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer directly through the App's website or support channel.

---

*Language Rally is designed with privacy by default: your learning data is yours alone.*
